Are You Eligible
To Apply to Our Home?


Use the following set of guidelines to determine if you are eligible to apply to be a resident in our home care.


  1. Age Requirement: I am at least 65 years of age.
  2. Residency Status: I am a resident in Brampton.
  3. Assistance Needs: I may require assistance with daily activities such as bathing, dressing, or medication management.
  4. Legal Capacity: I have the legal capacity to make decisions regarding my residence.
  5. Documental Proofs: I have access to the following necessary documents:
    • Passport
    • Proof of past income (Payroll or T4 statement)
    • Medical record (if any medical condition exists)
  6. Family Contact: I have contacts of at least two family members, including the following:
    • First and Last Name
    • Phone Number
    • Email
    • Adress
    • Current Employment
  7. Legal Residency Status: I am willing to satisfy the legal residency status required by the facility.

If you are able to satisfy all seven guidelines, you are eligible to become one of our welcomed residents!
